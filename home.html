Mission Statement

Our mission is to reduce obesity rates and improve the health of individuals by promoting healthy lifestyle choices, creating accessible and affordable opportunities for physical activity, and increasing access to nutritious food options.

About

This website utilizes the US Department of Agriculture FDC API and we have made an algorithm and calculation that grabs your weight, diet, gender, height, and age to evaluate your metabolic rate. The metabolic rate calculates your future weight to then assess your life expectancy.
